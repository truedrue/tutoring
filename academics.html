-Academic Support-

Most often I support students in their Math and/or Science classes, but often what is on the student’s plate at a given time will dictate what the top priority is. I've helped students with all classes, including electives such as Chinese, Computer Science, and Latin.

Students enjoy working with me because:

  • With one-on-one tutoring, there is no "class" that has to move on to stay on schedule. My job is to meet the student where they are and keep presenting content until it clicks. 
  • I believe a love of learning is caught and not taught. I completely empathize with those who have zero interest in a subject (been there, done that), but my enthusiasm often encourages my students to become more engaged than they would on their own.

I believe quality teaching requires camaraderie with students as well as a high level of professionalism. I often work collaboratively with student's parents & teachers. I am highly organized & value efficiently. My love of learning, my warm, positive attitude, & my extensive experience in education and tutoring make me a go-to tutor for many families. I'd love to meet and work with yours!
