-Academic Support-

Most often I support students in their Math and/or Science classes, but often what is on the student’s plate at a given time will dictate what the top priority is.

Yes, standardized tests are an unfortunate part of education, but they can also be fun. At least, I think so! Test prep is half about reviewing/learning actual content, but the rest is all problem-solving. Having a good strategy and confidence can make such a difference. 

I help my students see improvements by:

  • Quizzing them early and often on content they NEED to know.
  • Developing a personalized test-taking strategy.
  • Ensuring they test the way they practice through regular "mock" exams.

Students enjoy working with me because I'm warm and enthusiastic about what I teach. I always do my best to meet them where they are at and build from there. Parents enjoy working with me because I'm highly organized and keep them in the loop with regular updates.
