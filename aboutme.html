Hi, I'm Drew

I love sharing my passion for learning through tutoring and have been at it for over a decade. My goal isn't just to help students pass a test - it's to build genuine understanding and confidence that carries forward.

My Favorite Students

My all-time favorite students are those who are stuck in a negative mindset:

"I'm no good at math."

"Chemistry is too hard for me."

I can fully empathize with these students, as I avoided math and science for many years of my life. I can also share my insights and passion for those same topics now that they are among my favorite things!

Genuine Empathy

Tailored Teaching

Warm & Enthusiastic

My Teaching Philosophy

Every student learns differently. Some need visual aids, others need hands-on practice, and some just need someone to patiently walk them through concepts step by step. I meet students where they are and adapt my methods to fit how they learn best.

Whether we're working on test prep or everyday academics, I focus on teaching transferable skills: how to approach problems, manage time, stay organized, and believe in their own abilities.
